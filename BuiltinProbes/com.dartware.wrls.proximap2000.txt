<description>
\GB\Proxim Access Point\P\

This probe monitors \u2=http://www.proxim.com\Proxim\p0\ AP-2000 access points.

The probe displays the device's general settings, wireless interface RF parameters, security information, and wireless clients. To show the wireless client list, set \i\Show station statistics\p\ to \bi\true\p\.

\b\Note:\p\ Some of the alarms/warnings of this probe will not function unless the Proxim device is set to send traps to the InterMapper server.

\b\Parameters\p\

\i\Many clients warning\p\, \i\Too many clients alarm\p\, 
\i\FCS errors/sec high warning\p\, \i\FCS errors/sec too high alarm\p\, 
\i\Failures/sec high warning\p\, \i\Failures/sec too high alarm\p\, 
\i\Retries/sec high warning\p\, \i\Retries/sec too high alarm\p\,
- enter thresholds for \b\Warning\p\ and \b\Alarm\p\.

\i\Show wireless if settings\p\ - choose whether to show wireless interface settings. 

\i\Show security settings\p\ - choose whether to show security settings. 

\i\Show station statistics\p\ - choose whether to show statistics.

</description>
